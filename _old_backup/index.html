About

Cloud-native infrastructure, secured end-to-end.

I'm a Cloud, Infrastructure & Security Engineer with over five years at LEAN Services, a private cloud service provider in Kuwait. I started in the Services Management Center (SMC) handling 24/7 operations and progressed to the Core Technology team, where I now lead cloud, virtualization, networking, and security implementations.

My day-to-day spans the full solution lifecycle — from architecture and HLD/LLD design, to deployment, performance tuning, DR readiness, and vendor escalation. I act as the technical focal point with major vendors (Lenovo, Palo Alto, Fortinet, VMware, Cohesity, Nutanix) and coordinate with every ISP in Kuwait for enterprise connectivity.

I also support pre-sales engagements: translating customer requirements into right-sized technical architectures, and presenting designs to enterprise clients across Kuwait.
