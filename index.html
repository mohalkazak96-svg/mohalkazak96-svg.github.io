Freelance Services

What I offer

Hands-on consulting for cloud, virtualization, and network security — from greenfield design to optimization of existing environments.

Private Cloud Architecture

VMware (vSphere, vSAN, NSX, Cloud Director) and Nutanix design, sizing, and deployment for IaaS, BaaS, and DRaaS workloads.

Network & Security Design

Enterprise switching, routing, and segmentation. Firewall deployment (Palo Alto, FortiGate, Sophos) and SDN micro-segmentation with NSX.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Cohesity backup design, DRaaS architecture, RPO/RTO planning, Cristie BMR for bare-metal recovery, and DR validation.

SD-WAN & Connectivity

VMware SD-WAN (VeloCloud) and Fortinet SD-WAN deployments. NSX Advanced Load Balancer (AVI) design and operations.

Pre-Sales Engineering

HLD/LLD documentation, BoM sizing, RFP responses, technical presentations, and customer-facing solution demonstrations.

Infrastructure Audit & Optimization

Performance and capacity reviews using vRealize, Zabbix, and Lenovo XClarity. Identify bottlenecks, right-size workloads, harden security posture.
